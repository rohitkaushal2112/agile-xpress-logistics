About Us

Who we are and what we stand for.

Agile Xpress Logistics Inc. is a Canadian-based logistics company dedicated to providing reliable, cost-effective freight and warehousing solutions across the country and beyond. Our team brings together expertise in less-than-truckload (LTL), full truckload (FTL), full container load (FCL), refrigerated transport, and warehousing to support businesses of all sizes.

We believe in building lasting partnerships with our clients by delivering on our promise of fast, reliable, and nationwide service. Whether you need a single shipment or ongoing logistics support, we are here to help you move your cargo efficiently and safely.
